The basic spot is the 20-yard line for fouls by either team when the opponent of the team in possession at the time of the foul is responsible for forcing the ball across the goal line of the team in possession and the related run ends in the end zone and is followed by a loose ball, regardless of where the loose ball becomes dead.

APPROVED RULING

AR 9.12.A

B1 intercepts A1's pass in B's end zone. B1 is grabbed by the facemask by A2. B1 then fumbles while in the end zone. the ball rolls back into the field of play and then (a)goes out of bounds at B's 2-yard line or (b) is recovered by B7 at B's 5-yard line. RULING: In both (a) and (b), the basic spot is the 20-yard line. The accepted foul will result in a first down for B at its 35-yard line.
