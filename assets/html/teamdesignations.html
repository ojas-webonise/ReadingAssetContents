The offense is the team that is in possession of the ball. The opponent is the defense.

A and B

A is the team that puts the ball in play. A player of A is A1 and teammates are A2 and A3. The opponent is B. A player of B is B1 and teammates are B2 and B3.

K and R

K is the team that legally kicks the ball during the down. A player of K is K1 and teammates are K2 and K3. The opponent is R. A player of R is R1 and teammates are R2 and R3.
