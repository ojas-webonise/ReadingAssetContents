A drop kick is a legal kick by a player who drops the ball and kicks it when it touches the ground or as it is rising from the ground. A drop kick may be used for a scrimmage kick, including a field goal or try; a kickoff; a free kick following a safety; or for a free kick following a fair catch or awarded fair catch.

In or Behind Neutral Zone

A may drop kick from in or behind the neutral zone before team possession has changed.
