If team K fouls during a scrimmage kick play, team R has the option of enforcement from the previous spot or from the end of the play.

Automatic First Down

If B is penalized for roughing the kicker, holder or passer; forward pass interference; or any personal foul, the penalty includes an automatic first down for A.

15-Yard Penalty from Previous Spot

Pass interference against either team results in a 15-yard penalty enforced from the previous spot.
